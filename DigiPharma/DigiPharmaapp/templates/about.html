About DigiPharma

DigiPharma is your one-stop solution for managing your pharmaceutical needs. Our application allows you to buy medicines conveniently online, manage prescriptions, and keep track of your sales and purchases.

Features

  • Easy browsing and purchasing of medicines
  • Prescription management for safe and valid purchases
  • Access to sales details and transaction history
  • Customer feedback and suggestion system
  • User-friendly interface for managing your profile
